🔮 History of Tarot Cards

Tarot cards have a long and mysterious history connected with spirituality, symbolism, and intuition. Today tarot is widely used for guidance, self -reflection, and spiritual insight.

🌙 Origin of Tarot

Tarot cards first appeared in the 15th century in Italy as playing cards for entertainment among wealthy families. These early tarot decks were called Tarocchi cards.

The oldest known tarot decks were hand-painted and used by royal courts.

🃏 Evolution Into Spiritual Practice

During the 18th century, tarot became associated with mysticism and occult traditions. Spiritual practitioners believed the cards contained hidden wisdom and symbolic messages connected to the universe and human consciousness.
